Film Favorites

I wanted to share some of my favorite stories that I have loved throughout the most recent years.

Top 3

"Sorry To Bother You" is about Cassius Green (Lakeith Stanfield) venturing off to his telemarketing job while trying to climb up the corporate ladder in a capitalistic society. He code-switches his voice and uses his "white voice" in order to become more successful. It is the weirdest movie you'll ever see and it exposes you to the realities of how capitalistic America works, especially for black Americans.

"Parasite" is about a poor family in Korea invading a rich South Korea family by taking jobs as the family's houseworkers. It explores the disparity between the rich and poor and trying to survive when you don't have much means to live on. The performances by Song Kang-ho and Park So-dam are phenomenal and it shows the realities of the poor as well as the rich.

"Before Midnight" is the last edition to the "Before" series in which, Jesse (Ethan Hawke) and Celeste (Julie Deply) have settled into married life and must work on the hidden problems in their marraige while vacationing in Greece.
